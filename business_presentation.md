## SLIDE 5: Customer Insights

**Title:** 76.5% Retention Rate — Focus on At-Risk Customers

**Story:** Most of our revenue comes from repeat customers. Retaining existing customers costs 5x less than acquiring new ones.

- Customer Retention Rate: 76.5%
- New Customers: 754 (this quarter)
- At-Risk Customers (no purchase in 60 days): 423
- Revenue at Risk: $287K
- Top 10% of customers = 45% of revenue

**Recommendation:** Launch a re-engagement email campaign for the 423 at-risk customers. Potential recovery: $150K-$200K.
